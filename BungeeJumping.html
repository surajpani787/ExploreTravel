Best BungeeJumping in India

1. Jumpin Heights in Mohan Chatti, Rishikesh

Jumpin Heights, located at Mohan Chatti in Rishikesh, is the highest bungee jumping spot in India. It is also regarded as the best bungee jumping location in India by adventurists. It is the only place in India where bungee jumping can be done from a fixed platform. A Cantilever platform is built over a rocky cliff which is 83 meters above ground level, making it the highest and the most thrilling bungee jumping location in India. Jumpin Heights is popular for its safety measures and experienced staff. Costing around Rs. ₹3550 per jump inclusive of GST, it is a bit expensive but totally worth the experience.

2. Ozone Adventures in Bangalore

Ozone in Bangalore is also one of the most popular bungee jumping locations in India. Conducted from the height of 80 feet above the ground, and unlike usual jumping locations, there isn’t any fixed platform. A 130-ft high mobile crane fastens the entire bungee jumping equipment here. Anyone between the age group of 18 and 60 years can enjoy this adrenaline-pumping experience at Ozone Adventures. Safety receives utmost importance here, even though suspended from a crane. You can assuredly leave the worries at home.

3. Wanderlust in Delhi

If you are looking for a good bungee jumping location in Delhi, Wanderlust would be your best bet. Trained in Germany, the entire bungee jumping staff at Wanderlust operates with German technology and equipment. It offers crane jumps from 130 ft above the ground level. Any adventure enthusiast between the age group of 14 to 50 years can perform this thrilling sport at wanderlust.

4. Gravity Adventure Zone in Goa

If you are planning to go to Goa any time soon you should definitely try bungee jumping at Gravity zone. It is around Anjuna beach, one of the most popular attractions in Goa. It has set up a permanent 25-meter-high tower for bungee jumping which costs around 500 rupees per jump. You can easily choose Gravity Zone to be your first bungee jumping location, all thanks to it’s lower height.

5. Della Adventure in Lonavala

Della Adventures in Lonavala is one of the largest adventure parks in India. It is also a popular bungee jumping destination in India. The jumping platform is 45 meters and the leap lasts for around 4 to 5 minutes. This one is for folks above the age of 10 and it’ll cost around 1500 per jump.
